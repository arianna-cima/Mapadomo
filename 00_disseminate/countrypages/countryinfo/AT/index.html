Sources and definitions

Headline price data sources for AT

Prices 2016-2019 are based on Statistik Austria prices per 18 different dweeling types and 3 size classes per district (as log averages). Each of these are attributed to the district municipalities, and than aggregated up to NUTS-3 level according to the total m2 per type (NUTS-3 levels and districtis do not match in Austria). Prices are projected backwards using OeNB indexes (for Vienna), respectly an imputation exercise based on immopreise.at (until 2013), and national sub-indices (until 2007).

Building stock data sources for AT

dwellings per type are taken directly from Statistik Austria building census data on municipalities, and aggregated up to NUTS-3 level.


Definition of house v apartment for AT

apartment is any dwelling within a building that contains 3 or more dwellings (base level census data).

Definition and data sources for square metres for AT

Square metre data from 2011 onwards is taken directly from from the annual building census.
