Sources and definitions

Headline price data sources for LT

2004 to 2019 prices are based on the average sales-purchase prices of dwelling, available at nuts3 level. These prices are weighted by stocks data by year at nuts2-1-0.

Offer price data sources for LT

2004 to 2019 prices are based on the webscraped data and are then extended backward and forward according to the hpi coming from the same statistical office www.osp.stat.gov.lt.
weighted by by stocks data by year at nuts2-1-0.

Building stock data sources for LT

dwellings and sqm from 2004 to 2021 are taken from the Statistical Office of Lituania (www.osp.stat.gov.lt).
Stock of dwellings comparable up to the year 2008. From 2009, different data source. From 2009, summerhouses included.

Definition of house v apartment for LT

house is any dwelling within a building that contains 1 or 2 dwellings.

Definition and data sources for square metres for LT

sqm have been calculated multiplying the number of dwellings for the average size of the class size.
