Sources and definitions

Headline price data sources for ES

2005 to 2019 prices are based on purchased data, available per municipalities collected from the Ministerio de Transportes, Movilidad y Agenda
Prices are weighted by the number of transactions at nuts3 level and by stocks data by year at nuts2-1-0.
The "Valor tasado medio de vivienda libre" is considered average overall price. The "Valor tasado medio de vivienda libre de los municipios mayores de 25.000 habitantes" was considered as Flat price. House prices is the residuals coming from the difference between overall total value and total value of flats

Building stock data sources for ES

dwellings and sqm of 2011 per type all stem from Instituto Nacional de Estadistica and Census Hub. Time series has been extended backward and forward (2007-2019) according to the construction data coming from www.mitma.gob.es. Time series has been extended backward to 2001 using data of the 2001 census (Official Instituto Nacional de Estadistica) and interpolation according to linear growth rate.

Definition of house v apartment for ES

house is any dwelling within a building that contains 1 or 2 dwellings.

Definition and data sources for square metres for ES

sqm have been calculated multiplying the number of dwellings for the average size of the class size.
