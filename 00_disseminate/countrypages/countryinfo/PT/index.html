Sources and definitions

Headline price data sources for PT

Prices for flats are based on the median square metre sales price for existing apartments as provided by ine.pt, by municipality. Data gaps are imputed based on the average square per square metre from bank valautions data (via ine.pt). Hous eprices are based on average square metre prices from bank valuation data (via ine.pt).

Building stock data sources for PT

Definition of house v apartment for PT

flat is any dwelling within a building that contains 3 or more dwellings (for stocks). For prices, flat prices refer to 'apartment'prices as labelled by ine.pt.

Definition and data sources for square metres for PT

Square metre is useful square metre as defined by ine.pt, census 2011. The number of square metres and rooms is computed based on the 2011 census (for occupied dwellings, with imputed adjusted averages for unoocipeid dwellings), and carried forward and backward using data on square metres in completed and destroyed dwellings (also from ine.pt).
